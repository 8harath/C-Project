About Us

Warehouse and Inventory Management System

A comprehensive pharmaceutical inventory management solution designed to streamline medicine tracking, sales recording, and analytical insights.

Our system enables role-based access for administrators and staff to efficiently manage medicine inventory, record sales transactions through barcode scanning, and generate valuable analytical reports. With features like low stock alerts, seasonal forecasting, and alternative medicine recommendations, we help pharmacies maintain optimal inventory levels and improve customer service.

Built with modern web technologies including Flask, SQLAlchemy, and Bootstrap, the system offers a responsive and intuitive user interface accessible from any device.

Key Features
  • Role-based Access Control
  • Medicine CRUD Operations
  • Barcode Scanning
  • Sales Recording
  • Low Stock Alerts
  • Analytics & Reports
  • Predictive Insights
  • Alternative Medicines
